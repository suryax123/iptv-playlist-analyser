Privacy Policy

At StreamNinja, we hold your privacy and data security in the highest regard. This privacy policy explains our philosophy regarding data collection, how our infrastructure handles your inputs, and exactly what happens to your network traffic when you use our M3U analyzer tool.

💡 TL;DR: We do not log, save, or track your IPTV playlists, stream tokens, passwords, or the URLs you test inside our platform.

1. Data Storage & Local Processing

When you submit an M3U playlist URL to the StreamNinja platform, our backend infrastructure fetches the text strictly into volatile memory (RAM) to parse the file structure for HTTP networking checks and tvg-id validation.

  • No Database Storage: We do not possess a database infrastructure connected to user inputs. Your M3U lists never get saved to a disk.
  • Client-Side Operations: Generation of your PDF analysis reports happens entirely inside your browser using JavaScript memory. The PDF file is rendered locally on your device; it is not created on or downloaded from our servers.

2. Usage Analytics

To continuously monitor server stability, rate limits, and DDOS attack prevention, we log standard web server telemetry (such as connection timestamps, anonymous IP address hashes, and aggregate HTTP error codes). Under no circumstances do these logs include the contents of the URLs or files you are running through our testing algorithms.

3. Cookies and Caching

StreamNinja does not deploy any tracking cookies, third-party marketing pixels (such as Facebook/Meta Pixels), or affiliate retargeting IDs. We optionally store Service Worker caching packets locally in your browser. This is done strictly to ensure our platform can load instantly upon your return and offer basic functionality and cached CSS without requiring excess network requests (PWA standard capability).

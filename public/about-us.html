About Us

Welcome to StreamNinja! We are a passionate team of developers dedicated to building the most reliable web-based tools for network diagnostics, IPTV stream analysis, and M3U playlist validation. We built this platform because we believe in providing network engineers, cord-cutters, and technical enthusiasts with an efficient, no-nonsense solution for testing stream connectivity and verifying M3U syntax without needing to install heavy desktop software.

Our platform is completely free, highly secure, and deeply respects your privacy. Unlike other analyzer tools, we do not store any of your data, passwords, or playlists on our servers. All parsing is processed securely and temporarily, leveraging live Server-Sent Events (SSE) to test your streams in real-time from memory. Once you close your browser, your data is gone forever.

What We Offer

🔍 Deep Analysis

Check 10,000+ channel statuses instantly, parse M3U metadata (`tvg-id`, `tvg-logo`), extract nested category groups, and get detailed insights on HTTP response codes like 403 or 503.

⚡ Lightning Fast

Experience zero browser timeouts. Our customized Node server runs parallel channel checking (up to 50 streams processed concurrently) using optimized batch streaming.

📄 PDF Reports

Instantly export detailed analysis reports or your customized clean playlists as beautifully formatted, offline-ready PDF documents generated client-side.
